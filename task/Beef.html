Beef


Beef meat, derived from cattle, is one of the most popular and widely consumed types of meat around the world. It is known for its rich flavor, tender texture, and versatility in cooking. Beef can be prepared in various forms, including steaks, roasts, ground beef, and stews. It is an excellent source of high-quality protein, essential vitamins like B12, and important minerals such as iron and zinc. Different cuts of beef offer a range of flavors and textures, from the tenderloin to the tougher cuts like brisket, which benefit from slow cooking methods.
